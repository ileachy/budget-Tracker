## <img id="about" src="https://github.com/teamjuli0/readme-badges/blob/main/themes/clean-dark/menu-categories/about.png?raw=true" style="height: 40px">

- The Budget Tracker app will help you keep track of where your money is going and allow you to stay on top of purchases and incoming money as well with a couter that keeps track of what you currently have.

## <img id="usage" src="https://github.com/teamjuli0/readme-badges/blob/main/themes/clean-dark/menu-categories/usage.png?raw=true" style="height: 40px">

- To use the Budget Tracker you must first clone the repositry, run "npm i" and then "npm start" and open your localhost:3001. Once on the running applicationg you can input you transaction data as you please and track your spending!
